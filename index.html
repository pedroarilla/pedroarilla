¡Hola! I’m an independent Type Designer and Type Director with a passion for words living between London and Zaragoza. I love helping all kind of brands worldwide to encapsulate typographically their voices.

I’ve been designing, writing, teaching and lecturing about typefaces for the last decade. I’ve worked as a Creative Director at Monotype and Fontsmith; I hold an MA in Typeface Design from the University of Reading (graduating with distinction); host one of the main podcasts and publications on typography in Spanish (Don Serifa); and co-organise and teach an international one-week type design workshop (Glíglifo).
