¡Hola! I’m Pedro Arilla, an independent designer with a passion for words.

I am interested in how typography plays a storytelling role in the intersection of culture, technology and sociology. I’ve been designing, writing, teaching and lecturing about typefaces for the last decade. I’ve worked as a Creative Director at Monotype and Fontsmith. I hold an MA in Typeface Design from the University of Reading (graduating with distinction); host one of the main podcasts and publications on typography in Spanish (Don Serifa); and co-organise and teach an international one-week type design workshop (Glíglifo).
