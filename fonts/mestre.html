Mestre

A German & Dutch inspired geometric sans-serif. Its solid and formal shapes are embedded with a discreet humanist flair resulting in a very versatile contemporary hybrid, and highly functional and flexible font for many of today’s editorial and corporate requirements.

Features

  • 8 weights: Hairline, Thin, Light, Regular, Medium, Bold, Black, Heavy.
  • 16 fonts: 8 uprights + 8 true italics.
  • 900+ glyphs: 40+ languages supported.
  • OpenType features: small caps, case-sensitive forms, lining, tabular & old-style figures, scientific superior/inferior figures, fractions, a set of arrows, etcetera.
  • Version: 1.2 — see log and license.
